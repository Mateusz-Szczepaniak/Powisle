Information about Apartment

An exclusive apartment in the center of Warsaw in Powiśle for rent for days, days, weeks and months.

A new, modern and stylish apartment in the center of Warsaw with an air-conditioned living room for large families or groups of friends (up to 8 people).

The apartment is located in Powiśle, next to the Vistula Boulevards and at the Royal Route, near Al. Ujazdowskie, Plac Trzech Krzyży, Nowy Świat and close to the stadium - PGE National Stadium. Absolutely great location for discovering the charms of Warsaw, both during the day and at night.

A few words about Apartment

The warm colors and modern finish of the apartment creates a friendly atmosphere.
The apartment is located on the 6th floor of the building, and the large windows provide a view of downtown Warsaw, including the Palace of Culture.
The TV has access to cable television with TV programs in Polish and English. In total, the apartment has 8 beds for adults or children.

The apartment consists of several rooms:


  • Living room: sofa bed, TV, dining table for 6 people and air conditioning
  • Bedroom 1: double bed + private bathroom with shower
  • Bedroom 2: two single beds
  • Bedroom 3: sofa bed
  • Bathroom: additional with a bathtub
  • Kitchen: ceramic hob, oven, coffee maker, kettle, dishwasher and a set of dishes
